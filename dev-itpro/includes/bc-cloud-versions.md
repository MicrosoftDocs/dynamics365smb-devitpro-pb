- From on-premises version 25 and later, migrate directly to [!INCLUDE[prod_short](prod_short.md)] online.
- From on-premises version 15 through 24, upgrade to at least version 25 on-premises before migrating to [!INCLUDE[prod_short](prod_short.md)] online.
- From on-premises version 14, you have two options:
  - **Full migration**: Upgrade to at least version 25 on-premises, then migrate to [!INCLUDE[prod_short](prod_short.md)] online.
  - **Reimplementation**: Use the [Business Central 14 Reimplementation tool](../administration/migrate-bc14-reimplementation.md) to migrate only essential data (master data, opening balances, and setup) directly to the cloud without upgrading on-premises first.
