# Prepare demonstration environments for Business Central

As a [!INCLUDE [prod_short](../developer/includes/prod_short.md)] reselling partner, use demonstration environments to show prospects the capabilities of Business Central. This article helps you choose the right demo environment approach.

## Compare demo environment approaches

Choose the approach that best matches your demo goals, control needs, and timeline.

| Approach | Best for | Duration | Control | Requires |
|----------|----------|----------|---------|----------|
| **[MDX demo environment](demo-environment-mdx.md)** | Repeatable, partner-controlled demos that combine Business Central with Microsoft 365 apps and services. Extend with Power Platform and Dynamics 365 integration demos | 90 days or one year | Full tenant control | Partner Center enrollment |
| **[Prospect trial](demo-environment-trial.md#add-a-business-central-trial-to-your-prospects-organization)** | Quick evaluation in the prospect's own organization; can convert to a paid subscription | Up to 60 days | Prospect-owned | Prospect email account |
| **[Sandbox environment](demo-environment-trial.md#demo-in-sandbox-environments)** | Tailored or side-by-side demo scenarios in your existing Business Central tenant | Unlimited | Full tenant control | Existing Business Central tenant |
